ABOUT ME

Hi, welcome to my portfolio website.
I am multi-disciplinary designer specialising in communication and industrial design.

My passion lies in creating designs that conveys and communicates messages with purpose.
I believe visual cues have strong connection with human perception and behaviour.
I aspire to be a socially responsible designer, contributing to a safer, more comfortable, and accountable world.

Despite being a maximalist, I strive to create minimalistic designs to ensure a clear message reaches the audience. I think about what captures attention and how to engage creatively to effectively communicate my ideas through colour, form, and designs.

Here, you will explore my world of design.
